Career

Santiago shot to prominence and fame in the year 1995 as a part of the hip hop duo, Capone-N-Noreaga and they produced the “The War Report” album, singles like “Top of New York,” “Illegal Life,” “Closer,” which were both commercial and critical successes. These hits also earned them a place on the top music charts, like the Billboard 200 and the Hot R&B.

Post Capone’s imprisonment in 1997, Noreaga had to go the solo way. He released his debut album as a solo rapper, “N.O.R.E.,” which became a bigger hit than C-N-N’s debut album, “The War Report.”

His next single, a collaboration with the production duo The Neptunes, “Superthug,” became an even bigger range in the hip-hop community. He then went on to release his solo albums, “Melvin Flynt – Da Hustler,” “God’s Favourite,” “Normality,” “Student of the Game,” “5E” one after the other.

In 2013, he announced that he should stop recording solo albums and shift his focus entirely to music production and execution. He is currently the chief executive officer of his own company, “Militainment Business.”

Awards & Achievements

The American rapper’s debut album, “N.O.R.E.” got certified platinum by the Recording Industry Association of America and his second album, “Melvin Flynt – Da Hustler” got certified gold by the same. He won the “Favourite Reissues” award for his album, “Illmatic” in the year 2014.
