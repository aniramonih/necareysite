She was born on October 4, 1986, in The Bronx, New York City, New York, USA.

What is her educational background?

She graduated from high school at age 12 and attended SUNY Purchase, where she studied drama and began her acting career.

What are some of her notable film roles?

She has starred in several notable films, including Couples Retreat, Get Him to the Greek, Peeples, and Fifty Shades of Black.

Has she done any TV shows?

Yes, she has appeared in TV shows like New Girl as Shelby and Black Jesus as Maggie. She has also made guest appearances on shows like Inside Amy Schumer and Psych.
