Suits Actress Sarah Rafferty And Husband Santtu Seppala's Eighteenth Year Anniversary! Know The Deta

 Posted on August 4, 2024  |  3 minutes  |  541 words  |  Patria Henriques

Sarah Rafferty wowed everyone during the Royal wedding of the Duchess of Sussex when she appeared in her navy blue attire. In the Royal ceremony, the Suits actress arrived alongside her husband, Santtu Seppala.

Her appearance at the wedding made it even more special for Megan, but there's another thing that she is celebrating this year. Her eighteenth wedding anniversary, yes, guys, Sarah and Santtu, have managed to set the benchmark for the new couples successfully. In the article below, let's know more about her married life with Santtu.

Her Husband is a Stock Analyst

Most of the time, you hear that an actress is walking down the aisle with an actor, but in this case, she tied the knot with a stock research analyst. Sarah exchanged wedding vows with Santtu on June 23, 2001, in a small ceremony.

Santtu is not in the showbiz industry because of which the couple might've kept their married life away from the public eyes. Mr. Seppala works as a stock analyst in New York at Lazard Freres & Company.

The Wedding Details

Neither Sarah nor Santtu has provided any details regarding their bridal ceremony. In an article posted in the New York Times in 2001, mentioned that Reverand Alfred Riendeau officiated the ceremony at the Roman Catholic Church in Greenwich.

There are not many pictures of her wedding day, but recently, on the eve of her eighteenth wedding anniversary, Sarah posted a photo of her wedding day. In the photo, she is wearing a strapless gown with a long tulle veil, and on the other hand, her husband is looking dashing in his black suit.

The picture also shows how happy the couple looks to marry each other. They are laughing and hugging each other, which suggests how their wedding might've concluded.

She Gave Her Daughter a Unique Name

In her long marital life with Santtu, she expanded her family from two to four. She is a proud mother of two children, both daughters with whom the actress spends a lot of time. Her Instagram page is filled with photos of her work and her daughters.

The amazing mother of two not just gave birth but also came up with a unique idea to their name. Her eldest daughter, Oona Gray Seppala's name, is derived from an Irish name Una. Oona was born in October 2007, six years after Sarah's wedding.

Oona is not the only one with a unique name; her sister Iris Friday also falls in the bracket. Even though Iris is not an uncommon name considering Oona but her surname Friday is something that the couple came up with. Her daughters are joining their mother in Toronto, Canada, where Sarah is currently shooting for the final season of Suits.

Whenever Sarah is busy with her work, Santtu spends time with his little girls. In many of the Instagram posts, we can see Santtu taking his daughters and his mother-in-law for an outing. Not just that, the couple, along with their daughters, take an extravagant vacation in an exotic destinations taking time from their busy schedules. Recently, Sarah did a horse ride through Solvang, California, while her hubby and her daughters prepare for Halloween.
