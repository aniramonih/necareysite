Who Is Iam Tongi?, Songs, Birthday, Family, Social Media: Iam Tongi is an 18-year-old singer who emerged as the winner of American Idol 2023.

Tongi is originally from Kahuku, a town in O’ahu, Hawaii, and is of Tongan, Samoan, and Irish descent.

He’s a senior at Decatur High School in Federal Way, Washington, outside of Seattle, where he and his family moved three years ago.

From his first audition, he won the judges’ hearts singing from his heart during the first round and impress viewers with his soulful style of singing.

In the grand finale of the 2023 American Idol, Iam Tongi delivered powerful renditions of Keith Urban’s “Making Memories of Us,” and “Cool Down,”.

He also performed a sentimental tribute to his late father titled “I’ll Be Seeing You.” Tongi also teamed up with James Blunt to perform “Monsters.”

During the grand finale, the 3 remaining contestants; Iam Tongi, Megan Danielle, and Colin Stough wowed viewers with solo performances and duets with renowned artists.

The star-studded event had in attendance; Keith Urban, Kylie Minogue, Ellie Goulding, Lionel Richie, TLC, REO Speedwagon, Pitbull, and Lil Jon, among others.

After nearly a four-hour live performance-filled show, 18-year-old Iam Tongi from Kahuku claimed the title of “American Idol.

His victory at the just-ended 2023 American Idol on Sunday, May 21 makes him the first person from the Hawaiian Islands to win “American Idol.”

He is also the first Pacific Islander to win the singing competition show, per the Asian American Legal Defense and Education Fund.

Tongi is now ready to make his own music after the historic victory and a recording contract. This season’s American Idol is over but another one is officially on the way for 2024.

Iam Tongi Birthday

Iam Tongi celebrated his 18th birthday in September last year (2022). He was born on September 1, 2004, in Kahuku, Hawaii. Tongi will be 19 in September this year (2023).

Iam Tongi Songs & Videos

Iam Tongi owns a YouTube channel with over 118,000 subscribers. His debut single captioned; I’ll Be Seeing You, is a tribute to his late father; Rodney Guy who died on December 28, 2021.

He has also recorded and shared several acoustic covers of popular songs on his YouTube channel.

Iam Tongi Family

Parents

Iam Tongi was born in Kahuku, Hawaii to his parents; Rodney Guy (father) and Lillie N. Rodney Tongi (mother).

His father who was also a musician, recognized Iam’s talent and supported his passion for singing. Unfortunately, his father, Rodney Guy died on December 28, 2021.

Wife

The 18-year-old singer is not married and hence does not have a wife. Iam Tongi is at the moment single and not dating anyone. He looks so much more focused on his career than dating for now.

Children

The 2023 American Idol winner, Iam Tongi is not yet a father. He does not have any biological or adopted children.

Siblings

Iam Tongi is not the only child of his parents; Rodney Guy (father) and Lillie N. Rodney Tongi (mother). He has two sisters; Cassandra Alvina and Leilei Tongi, and two brothers, Lerod. The name of his other brother is unknown.

Iam Tongi hardly shares information regarding his family hence the dates of birth, ages, and occupations of his other siblings were unknown at the time of this writing
