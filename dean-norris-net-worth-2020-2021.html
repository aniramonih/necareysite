Early Life & Biography

Dean Norris was born on 8th April 1963 in South Bend, Indiana, the United States to parents Rosemarie Norris and Jack B. Norris. He owned a furniture shop in Indiana. Norris has two sisters, elder Connie and younger, Nikie.

Norris graduated from Clay High school in South Bend in 1981, where he was a class valedictorian. In 1985< he graduated from Harvard College with a major in Social Studies. He also attended of Royal Academy of Dramatic Art in London for one year.
