Keean Bexte Counter Signal Journalist; Wiki, Bio, Age, Fired Rebel News &amp; Twitter

 Posted on August 22, 2024  |  2 minutes  |  282 words  |  Martina Birk

Keean Bexte is an independent journalist based in Alberta Canada and is the founder and Editor in Chief of The Counter Signal, a mainstream media alternative, news, and culture podcast.  He identifies as a far-right conservative. He has in the past worked for three years at Rebel News. The Alberta-based journalist said he may return from time to time to work on individual journalistic projects.

Keean Bexte Age

He is in his mid-20s based on his year of graduation from the University.

Keean Bexte Education

Bexte is a graduate of the Univesity of Calgary with a Bachelor of Science (B.Sc.), Natural Sciences, Minor in Economics. He attended the institution from 2013 to 2018 and was part of the University of Calgary Campus Conservative Association.

Keean Bexte Career

Prior to his career at The Counter Signal, Bexte worked as a Video Journalist for the Rebel News Network Ltd in Calgary, Alberta from Sep 2018 to Mar 2021. Rumors indicate that he was fired from that position In his farewell post, he wrote, ‘It has been a delight working with this team over the years, and I know Rebel will continue sharing the other side of the story without me.’

He has also worked as a Research Assist at the House of Commons of Canada from May 2017 to Aug 2017 and as a Ministerial Staff for Agriculture and Agri-Food Canada in Ottawa from May 2014 o Sep 2014.
