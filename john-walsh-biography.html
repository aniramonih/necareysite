Who is John Walsh?

John Walsh is an American television personality, criminal investigator, human and victim rights advocate, host, and creator.

He is best known for ‘America Fights Back’ (1998), ‘The Hunt with John Walsh’ (2014), and ‘Springfield’s Most Wanted’ (1995). Also, he is popularized for his anti-crime activism.

Age, Parents, Siblings, Family, Ethnicity

Walsh was born on December 26, 1945, in Auburn, New York, the U.S. His nationality is American and Irish-American descent.

He was born to American parents. His mother’s name is Revé Drew Walsh and his father’s name is unknown. He was raised up with his parents in the same place where he was born. He has a sister named Meghan Walsh.
