Jersey Shore alum Sammi Giancola unfollowed her fiance Christian Biscardi on Instagram. That gave the spark to the split-up rumors after two years of their engagement.

Moreover, the Jersey Shore star also ditched her engagement ring after the rumors of their split. Are the couple going their separate ways after getting engaged for two years? Though they have not officially announced anything they are also giving shuttle hints.

Are Sammi Giancola and Christian Biscardi splitting up?

The Jersey Shore star and Christian dated for two years before he proposed to her in March 2019. Meanwhile, they recently made headlines for their splitting rumors. Fans noticed they have unfollowed each other on Instagram.

Moreover, she also ditched her engagement ring which gave fire to their split-up rumors. Sammi also deleted all the pictures of them from the past two years.

The last picture they posted together was in March 2020. Giancola last-mentioned Biscardi on her YouTube account was in December when she talked about her favorite holiday tradition.

The couple hasn’t reached out for any comments at the current time. They started dating back in 2017 and got engaged in 2019. The couple had planned their wedding in 2020 which got delayed back due to the COVID-19 pandemic.

At the time, she posted a picture of him bent down the knee while putting a ring on her finger. Alongside the picture, she wrote that was the best day of her life and looking forward to spending her life with him.

Wedding plans of the couple

Before their split-up rumors, she was all set to walk down the aisle for her ‘I DO’ ceremony with Christian. Last year in 2020, she announced that she said ‘YES’ to her wedding dress to her fans and followers. However, she kept the style of the gown and any further details secret at the time.

The reality TV personality took her fans inside the boutique through her YouTube video. She interviewed  Castle Couture’s Bridal Manager to give women tips for finding the perfect dress for their big day.

After the video, she stayed away from the spotlight and also did not come to the Jersey Shore Family Vacation. She also said she wanted “to avoid potentially toxic situations.” Before meeting Christian, she was dating Ronnie Ortiz-Magro from 2009 until 2012.

He got arrested for domestic violence following the incident with his new girlfriend Saffire Matos. After she said she was avoiding the toxic situation, many fans assumed she was referring to Ortiz.
