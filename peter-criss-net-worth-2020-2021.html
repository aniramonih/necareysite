Early Life

Peter Criss was born on December 20, 1945, in Brooklyn, New York. He is the son of Loretta and Joseph Criscuola. He has four younger siblings. The famous drummer Jerry Nolan was also his childhood friend. Criss was very passionate about music from a very young age. He was a huge fan of Gene Krupa and his exceptional style of drumming.

Criss was a passionate art student and was also used to play with the bandleader Joey Greco. Later, he got the chance to learn from his mentor Gene Krupa at the Metropole Club. After that, he started his musical journey by playing jazz and rock with several bands of New York and New Jersey.

Career

In the early stage of his career, he worked with many different bands. At the very same time, he joined the Chelsea band, who later released their first self-titled album in 1971. After the downfall of Chelsea, also known as Lips, he met with Paul Stanley and Gene Simmons, who was looking for a new band member.

In 1973, Criss formed a rock band named “Kiss” with the help of Paul Stanley, Gene Simmons, and Ace Frehley. The band immediately rose to fame with their energetic music, painted faces with unique costumes. They released their first self-titled album in 1974, in which Criss was the lead singer of several hit songs like “Beth,” “Black Diamond,” and “Hard Luck Woman.”

His song “Beth” was the highest-charting song till the date in the US and gathered the seventh position on Billboard’s Hot100. His song was also ranked at #3 positions in the VH1’s 25 Greatest Power Ballads.

He also released his solo albums, but that didn’t prove to be a good idea. He also tried his luck in acting and has appeared in films like “Kiss Meets the Phantom of the Park” and “Detroit Rock City.” Criss also got the chance in television series like “Oz,” “Frame of Mind,” and “Millennium.”

Awards & Achievements

Peter Criss has won many nominations and awards in different categories for his tremendous work. His blockbuster hit song “Beth” had won People’s Choice Awards in 1977. His solo album “Peter Criss” was awarded gold certification by Music Canada, where RIAA certified his album with platinum certification. In 2014, he was also introduced into the Rock and Roll Hall of Fame with the other members of Kiss.
