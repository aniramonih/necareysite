Early Life

Stephen Gary Wozniak, also nicknamed “The Woz”, commonly known as “Steve Wozniak” was born on August 11, 1950, in San Jose, California. He was born to his father, Jacob “Jerry” Wozniak, and mother, Margaret Louise Wozniak. His father was from Michigan while his mother was from Washington state. He is believed to be of Polish, English, German and Irish descent.

Wozniak attended Homestead High School and was an extremely bright and intelligent student who was a technology enthusiast. Right from his childhood, he loved electronics, and he often made himself occupied making homemade devices like a voltmeter, calculator, radio, etc. He hardly did any outdoor activities, like playing outdoor sports.

After graduating from high school, Wozniak flunked out of the University of Colorado, so he enrolled himself at the University of California. He graduated with a degree of BS in Electrical Engineer and Computer Science.

Wozniak always credited watching ‘Star Trek’ and attending the ‘Star Trek’ convention as a youth inspired him to come up with the idea of founding ‘Apple Inc’.

Career

It was through a common friend, Wozniak met Steve Jobs, who like him, hated school and had a passion for electronics. Soon, the duo became very good friends while working together on a mainframe computer at Hewlett-Packard.

Meanwhile, the demand for the personal computers escalated and hence the development of microprocessors. Wozniak couldn’t afford to buy a computer, so he built one, eventually followed by the first generation of ‘Apple Computers’ and titled ‘Apple I’. The ‘Apple I’ was fully designed by Wozniak, right from the hardware, circuit board designs to the operating system.

‘Apple I’ became the first home computer to display a character on a TV screen in the history of computing. Steve Jobs then took the responsibility of marketing, while Wozniak was busy augmenting their current ‘Apple’ model. What made ‘Apple I’ better from the rest was the fact that it had an easy-to-achieve video capability.

Wozniak also co-founded Wheels of Zeus to create wireless GPS technology in order to help an average person to find a way to their desired destination. Wozniak left the ‘Apple’ as he didn’t want to be a part of the management anymore. He then provided all the money in an organisation formed by him to support his educational and philanthropic projects.

Steve Wozniak is one the brilliant person who gave the world ‘Apple’ computers. He has been awarded numerous awards for his contribution to the world of electronics and computers. His biggest achievement would be the time when he was inducted into the ‘National Inventors Hall of Fame’.
