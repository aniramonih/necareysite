Maria Perroni Hails From a Long Line of Entertainers

She was born on July 20, 2008, in Mexico to Mariana Garza and Pablo Perroni. She has a step-sister named Shamadhi Alarcon whom her mother had from a previous marriage. Going through her family history, many of her family members made a name in the entertainment industry whether as actors, singers, or poets.

Her parents are successful entertainers who have spent decades crafting their works. Maria’s father is notable for his role in Vecinos(2005) and I Don’t Trust Men Anymore(2014). Her mother, Mariana is one of the successful Mexican actresses notable for her movie roles as well as her time with the band Timbiriche.

Other members of her family that are notable include:

  • Ana Silvia Garza-an actress and her maternal grandmother
  • Carmen Garza-a poet and her maternal great grandmother
  • Sebastian Garza-a journalist, actor, and her maternal uncle
  • Jaime Garza-Her maternal great uncle and actor
  • Maite Perroni-actress, singer, and her father’s cousin

She Started Acting at an Early Age

Considering that she grew up among entertainers and parents that are movie makers, it was not long before she also took the same career path. From her social media updates, it is clear that she started in the theatre at an early age. Her mother revealed in an interview that she started auditioning for roles at the age of 6

Per IMDB, her first role is in the telenovela series Vencer la Ausencia. She appeared in 80 episodes of the series. While her debut indicates she is new in the industry, Maria has proven that she is different through her skills both on screen and on stage.

Noticeably, she has shared the screen with her mother in the mentioned series above. While the mother-daughter pair have a close relationship in real life, it did not go unnoticed by fans that their screen role was the opposite of this.

Also, they acted alongside Benjamin Kant, her mother’s instructor of over 34 years. Apparently, getting instruction from Kant has extended to the rising Maria as she is also tutored by him.

The Celebrity Daughter is also a Skilled Singer

Aside from being an actress, Maria has an interest in music. She is credited with the 2018 soundtrack Da un Pasito Luminoso. The song is from the original banda sonora album contributed to by various artists.

From our findings, this is her only released material at the moment. Nonetheless, she has shared covers of songs on her social media. While she invests more in her acting career, it won’t be surprising if she pushes her music career.

Her Parent’s Marriage Lasted for 13 Years

As mentioned earlier, her parents are acclaimed actors yet they had a celebrity marriage that gained a lot of attention. Maria’s parents, Pablo and Mariana got married in 2006. Prior, her mother was married to Benjamin Alarcon as mentioned earlier.

Like her first marriage, the second also ended in 2019 after 13 years together. While fans were surprised about their divorce, it turns out that Pablo is bisexual. Since he is not the first celebrity to fall in love with both men and women, his was spectacular because he allegedly cheated on Maria’s mother with other men.

Since their divorce, they still maintained a cordial relationship afterward. They partner in their production works at the Teatro Milan and Foro Lucerna in Mexico City.

Maria Perroni’s Love Life

Considering that there is usually so much buzz around celebrity love life, her case is not different. The 15-year-old is in a romantic relationship with a man named Diego Enriques. The pair often share their loved-up images on social media whether while hanging out at public places or sharing private moments at their favorite places. Regarding what Diego does for a living, his Instagram Bio describes him as an artist. As it is, details about his career remain unclear.

The pair have been together since 2021. She revealed their one-year anniversary in a November 2022 Instagram post. Since then, the pair have become inseparable if their social media update is anything to go by.
