Is Belal Muhammad of Arab Ethnicity? Tune in to find out.

Belal Muhammad is a mixed martial artist. He began his professional career in 2012 and has formerly been a part of Bellator and Titan FC. Likewise, he joined UFC in 2016.

Muhammad’s debut UFC match was against Alan Jouban. He, however, lost his first professional match, but for their excellency, Alan and Belal received Fight of the Night honors.

Also known as ‘Remember The Name’, Muhammad has played a total of 23 matches. He has won 19 of them and lost 3 of them. Similarly, due to an accidental eye poke, Belal was not able to continue a match on March 13, 2021. Hence, it was a No Contest.

Following his fame, fans are curious about Belal Muhammad’s life. Let us learn about the Mixed Martial Artist in detail.

What is Belal Muhammad Ethnicity?

Belal Muhammad’s ethnicity is Arab. If he wins a UFC world title, Belal will be recognized as the first Arab UFC champion. In fact, he is looking forward to making history.

Discover Belal Muhammad Parents And Religion

Belal Muhammad’s parents are Moulavi Muhammad Yaqub and Mahmuda Khanom.

Belal’s father’s business was affected during the George Floyd Riots, as it was damaged by some of the protestors. He reported this incident to the public through a Twitter post, where he has uploaded a picture of his family shop.

Moving on, He attended Bogan High School. For his undergraduate studies, he joined the University of Illinois at Urbana-Champaign.

On the other hand, Belal Muhammad follows Islam as his religion and is a Muslim.

What Is Belal Muhammad Nationality: Where Is The MMA Fighter From?

Belal Muhammad was born and raised in Chicago, Illinois, and is of American Nationality. His parents, however, are from Al-Bireh, Palestine.

Additionally, Belal has not shied away from showing his support for Palestine. Not only has he denounced anti-Semitic responses from protesters but has also displayed the Palestinian flag during the beginning and end of his fights.

On the contrary, Belal will be facing Stephen Thompson on December 19, 2021. He has not lost the last six matches and is working towards keeping his winning streak.
