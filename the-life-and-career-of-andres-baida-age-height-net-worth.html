Who is Andres Baida?

Andres is a Mexican-American actor, probably best known to the world as Pepe Toledo in the TV series “Like, la Leyenda” (2018), and as Rodolfo in the TV series “Who Killed Sara?” (2021-2022), in addition to many other roles that he’s had so far in his career.

Andres Baida Wiki: Age, Childhood, and Education

Andres Baida was born on 28 February 1995 in El Paso, Texas USA. Of Mexican ancestry, Andres’s parents are originally from Ciudad Juárez. Unfortunately, he hasn’t revealed more information about his family, including his parents’ names and professions, nor has he talked about having siblings. He studied finance in the US, but left the program halfway to enroll in the Centro de Educación Artística de Televisa (CEA). There, he spent three years studying and participating in various theatrical productions.

Career Beginnings and Instant Fame

After graduating from CEA, he made his on-screen debut in the telenovela “Comi Dice el Dicho” (2018), following which Andrés took on the role of Pepe Toledo in the 2018–2019 telenovela “Like, la Leyenda“. Alongside Alejandro Belmonte, Mauricio Abad and Santiago Achaga, the series unfolds within LIKE, a prestigious private high school for the elite. Exploring the lives of a diverse group of young people, each with a unique story, the show tackles the challenges and experiences of adolescence.

The series received positive reviews for its portrayal of the youth and their relatable experiences, earning one award win and 13 other award nominations. This helped Andres a lot in his later career, as new popular roles kept coming his way.

He then took on the lead role of Hector in the telenovela “Los elegidos” (2019). Starring alongside Jason Romo, Maximiliano Uribe, and Sara Maldonado in all 34 episodes, and receiving praise for his performance. The series follows a group of children and adolescents with supernatural abilities, as they take a family guise to hide from the military that wants to exploit their powers.

The following year, Andrés took on the role of Pablo in the TV series “Control Z“, created by Miguel García Moreno, Adriana Pelusi and Carlos Quintanilla. Starring alongside Ana Valeria Becerril, Michael Ronda and Yankel Stevan, the show spanned 24 episodes, centered on Sofía, a student who takes action when a hacker starts revealing secrets in the high school.

Continued Success

Continuing his career, Andrés took on the role of young Rodolfo in the TV series “Who Killed Sara?” (2021–2022), spanning 23 episodes. Created by José Ignacio Valenzuela and starring Manolo Cardona, Ginés García Millán and Carolina Miranda, the series revolves around Álex’s quest for revenge and justice after being framed for his sister Sara’s murder. The story unfolds as enemies become allies, and Álex seeks answers to the lingering question about what truly happened to Sara.

Following this, Andrés joined the cast of “Los ricos también lloran” (2022), portraying Tomasito Verón Ortega in the 12-episode series. Starring Claudia Martín, Sebastián Rulli and Fabiola Guajardo, the show tells the story of Mariana, a woman from humble beginnings who moves into a luxurious mansion, and must navigate class differences when she falls in love with the patriarch’s son Luis Alberto.

Most recently, Baida took on the role of Rodrigo Carvajal Rivero in the TV series “Mi Secreto” (2022–2023), spanning 120 episodes. Starring alongside Macarena García Romero and Diego Klein, the series follows Valeria, who assumes the identity of her supposedly deceased best friend Natalia to avoid jail for a crime she didn’t commit.

Currently, Andrés Baida is immersed in his work on the upcoming TV series “Como Agua Para Chocolate“. While the plot details remain undisclosed, the drama series is slated to premiere in 2024, and features a stellar cast including Irene Azuela, Azul Guaita, Ana Valeria Becerril and Andrea Chaparro.

In addition, Andrés is currently working on the TV series “Bandidos”, which is currently in production with no specific release details available. This action-packed series features an ensemble of stars, including Sarah Nichols, Ester Expósito and Luis Gerardo Méndez. Set in the world of international treasure hunting, the plot revolves around a group of daring individuals striving to execute the ultimate heist.
