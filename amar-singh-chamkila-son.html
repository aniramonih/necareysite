Amar Singh Chamkila was an Indian singer and musician of Punjabi music. He is regarded as one of the best 5 live stage performers that Punjab has ever produced. He is considered one of the most influential Punjabi artists of all time.

His songs were related to Punjabi village life. He wrote songs about extra-marital relationships, coming of age, drinking, drug use, and men’s hot tempers. His best songs are “Pehle Lalkare Naal”, “Baba Tera Nankana”, “Talwar Main Kalgidhar Di”, “Jatt Di Dushmani”, and “Takue Te Takua”.
