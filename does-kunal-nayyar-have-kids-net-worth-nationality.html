Who Is Kunal Nayyar Married To? 

Kunal Nayyar is married to Neha Kapur, a former Miss India.

Kunal and Neha married each other in 2011 in New Delhi. India. According to the reports, the couple married in Hindu tradition, and the wedding lasted for over a week. 

The couple has been married for over a decade now, but they are not parents to any kids. Maybe in the future, we might hear some good news. 

Earlier, Kunal and his wife resided in California, United States. But after the Television series The Big Bang Theory ended, the couple has shifted back to Britain. 

Kunal Nayyar’s Nationality 

Kunal Nayyar is British by nationality. He was born in Hammersmith, London, United Kingdom.

Kunal was born into an Indian Immigrants family, they used to live in London, But the family shifted back to India when the actor was four years old. 

After The actor’s family returned to India, Kunal did his schooling at St.Columba’s School New Delhi, India.

Kunal is a Hindu by religion and stated his page on Instagram in which he talks about spirituality.
