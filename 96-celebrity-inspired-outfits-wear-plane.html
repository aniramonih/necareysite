Celebrity-Inspired Outfits to Wear on a Plane

 Posted on August 5, 2024  |  14 minutes  |  2928 words  |  Tandra Barner

Pre-Covid days, you better believe that I was showing up to the airport in sweats or leggings. That was my standard, hassle-free outfit for a comfortable plane ride. But post-vaccine and booking my first flight in over a year and I'm ready to wear an actual outfit to the airport — AND getting bags that match.

Why, you ask? Well I now view traveling as the privilege it's always been, and it's time to dress accordingly. Going to the airport means going someplace else and that in itself deserves a look.

I know the celebrities agree with me. Just look at Bella Hadid's early Ed Hardy-esque two-piece plane outfit or Kourtney Kardashian's silk, tea-length dress that she wore for a meaningful excursion with boyfriend Travis Barker. To provide you with ample inspiration for your post-vax airport outfits (get your vaccine, people!), we've rounded up 67 of the best celebrity airport outfits. See who supports leggings on the plane and who flies dressed to the nines every time.
