Career

His career as a ring announcer began in 1982. He started announcing all the boxing matches which were promoted on ESPN by Bob Arum. In 1984, he owned a trademark for the catchphrase “Let’s get ready to rumble”. In the 1980s, he became an exclusive ring announcer of all the bouts in the casinos owned by Donald Trump.

He was the former ring announcer of WCW (World Championship Wrestling). He left WCW in 2001 after it was folded by Time Warner group. He returned to be the ring announcer of professional wrestling in August 2007. He has also appeared in the commercial of the 2008 WWE Royal Rumble.

Michael Buffer has appeared in several movies as a ring announcer including ‘Creed’, ‘Grudge Match’, ‘You Don’t Mess with the Zohan’, ‘Rocky Balboa’, ‘Game Over’, ‘Ready to Rumble’, ‘Rocky V’, and ‘Harlem Nights’.

He has guest appeared in several TV shows like ’30 for 30’, ‘America’s Next Top Model’, ‘The Bold and the Beautiful’, ‘American Idol’, ‘Phineas & Ferb’, ‘Celebrity Boxing’, ‘Mad About You’, and ‘HBO Boxing’.
