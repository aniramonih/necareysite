Introduction to the Show

Jersey Shore was an American reality television series that aired on MTV from 3 December 2009 to 20 December 2012. The show was about eight-member living inside a vacation home and about their lives staring Sally Ann Salsano, Paul DelVecchio, Nicole Polizzi, Michale Sorrentine, Samantha Giancola, Ronnie Ortiz-Magro, Jennifer Farley, Vinny Guadagnino, Angelina Pivarrnick, and Deena Nicole Cortese.

The show consisted of six seasons out of four (1, 3, 5, and 6), which was shot in Seaside Heights, New Jersy, the second season in South Beach, Florida, and fourth season in Florence, Italy. The show faced some critical remarks by the locals when it was shot in Italy, especially for using the term ‘Guido/Guidette’ and stereotypes. The show was considered as one of the most notable shows of its time by the media, and soon the show became a pop culture phenomenon after the release of its first season.

After the show ended, four of its cast members appeared in other MTV shows the most popular being Snooki and JWOWW and other featured in reality shows that aired on different channels. On April 5, the entire cast was reunited in an MTV series ‘Jersey Shore: Family Vacation.’
