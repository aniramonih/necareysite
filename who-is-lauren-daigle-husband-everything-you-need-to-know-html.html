Early Life and Career

Lauren Daigle, the incredibly talented contemporary Christian music artist, was born on September 9, 1991, in Lafayette, Louisiana, USA. Her beginnings were humble, growing up with her father, mother, and two siblings in a close-knit family of five. Her passion for singing ignited at a tender age, as she immersed herself in Gospel music, Cajun, Blues, and Zydeco melodies.

At just 15, Lauren faced a tough battle with a viral infection called Cytomegalovirus, which weakened her immune system and confined her to her home for a lengthy two-year period. This challenging situation didn’t deter her spirit, as her mother enrolled her in voice lessons, and she eventually found her place in the church choir. In 2013, her solo journey began with a record deal that paved the way for her debut album, “How Can It Be,” a resounding success.
