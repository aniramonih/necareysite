Early Life & Education

Now 32 years old as of 2023, Leigh-Anne Pinnock was born on October 4, 1991, in High Wycombe, Buckinghamshire, England.

Growing up in a musically inclined family, Pinnock developed a deep passion for music from a young age. She was raised by her parents, Deborah Thornhill and John L. Pinnock, alongside her two sisters, Sarah and Sian-Louise, and her brother, Jonathan.

Her parents’ support and encouragement played a significant role in shaping her musical journey.

Pinnock spent her early years in High Wycombe, known for its vibrant music scene. She attended Sir William Ramsay School, where she discovered her love for performing arts.

Career

Leigh-Anne Pinnock‘s career took off when she auditioned for the reality TV show “The X Factor” in 2011. Alongside her bandmates, Jade Thirlwall, Perrie Edwards, and Jesy Nelson, Pinnock formed the girl group Little Mix. Their exceptional harmonies and dynamic performances quickly gained them a loyal fan base.

Little Mix went on to win “The X Factor” and signed a record deal with Syco Music. Their debut album, “DNA,” was released in 2012 and became a massive success, reaching the top of the UK Albums Chart. The album showcased Pinnock’s versatile vocals and began a remarkable journey for the group.

Over the years, Little Mix released hit singles like “Black Magic,” “Shout Out to My Ex,” and “Woman Like Me,” solidifying their status as one of the most influential girl groups in the industry. Pinnock’s soulful voice and powerful performances contributed to the group’s success.

In addition to her music career, Pinnock made her acting debut in the 2021 film “Boxing Day.” Her portrayal of the character Melody captivated audiences and showcased her talent beyond the world of music.

With her foray into acting, Pinnock continues to explore new horizons and push the boundaries of her artistic abilities.
