Tatyana Ali, who is currently self-isolating with her husband and two sons, was in reminiscing spirits as she took to Instagram to share a picture of her and her sisters.

With the novel coronavirus continuing to spread rapidly across the globe, many people have been forced to self-isolate, thereby limiting their access to friends, family members, and colleagues.

Many people have been forced to maintain communication with their loved ones over the internet and through their social media platforms. And favorite celebrities are not an exception to this new trend.

Talented actress Tatyana Ali recently took to social media to share a throwback picture of herself with her two sisters looking stunning in carnival costumes.

"Until we Slay together in person again!!! #siblings #trinitodabone #panamericans @thesealis @k_natasha_ali”

Tatyana Ali welcomed her second child Alejandro Vaughn with her husband, Dr. Vaughn Rasberry, on August 15, 2019. The couple shared the exciting news with their fans, expressing their joy over the new addition to their family.

Little Alejandro is the first sibling to the couple's three-year-old son Edward Aszard who they welcomed, back in September 2016.

Much drama was generated over the relationship between the late boxing legend Muhammad Ali and Tatyana Ali.

The 41-year-old actress met her husband, Dr. Vuaghn Rasberry, back in 2015, on popular dating website, eHarmony. The lovebirds exchanged letters for months before they finally met in person.

In 2016, the couple announced their engagement after Vaughn popped the question during a hike in California's Yosemite National Park. Speaking about her engagement, Ali told ET:

"Just the two of us. It was so thoughtful and perfect. We're best friends."

Ali got married to her beau in July 2016 at the Four Seasons in Beverly Hills, California. The ceremony was classy and glamorous and well attended by the family and friends of the couple.

The wedding, which was inspired by Ali's Caribbean history, also served as a reunion for her "Fresh Prince" co-stars — Alfonso Ribeiro, Karyn Parsons, Barbara Avery (Widow of James Avery), and Joseph Marcell.

In 2016, much drama was generated over the relationship between the late boxing legend Muhammad Ali and Tatyana Ali, with some speculating she may be one of his many secret children.

Those rumors were proven to be nothing but unfounded stories as there is no relationship whatsoever between the two, except for their similar names.
