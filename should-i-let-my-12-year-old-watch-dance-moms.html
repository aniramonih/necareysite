Should I let my 12-year-old watch Dance Moms?

Considering the content of Dance Moms, it may not be appropriate for a 12-year-old to watch. The show is not intended for children, as it contains controversial and mature themes. The conversations between the moms in the show are not child-friendly, as they often discuss their dissatisfaction with their appearances. Additionally, the show may not offer much substance for older viewers. However, it does provide entertainment value due to its controversial nature and explosive personalities.
