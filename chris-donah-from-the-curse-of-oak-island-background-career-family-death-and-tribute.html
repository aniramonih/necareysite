What is The Curse of Oak Island? 

The Curse of Oak Island is a reality television series that follows a group of treasure hunters as they explore Oak Island in quest of historical artefacts or “treasure.” The team is led by brothers Marty and Rick Lagina. 

There are many various theories about the hidden treasures on the island, which is known as a “Money Pit” and is off the coast of Nova Scotia, Canada. 

Dan and Dave Blankenship, a father and son team who have lived permanently on Oak Island and have been looking for treasure since the 1960s, accompanied Marty and Rick. On March 17, 2019, Dan Blankenship passed away at the age of 95.
