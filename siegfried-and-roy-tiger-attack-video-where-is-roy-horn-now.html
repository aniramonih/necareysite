Siegfried & Roy were a magician and entertainer pair who were well renowned for their appearances with white lions and white tigers. Siegfried Fischbacher, born on June 13, 1939, and Roy Horn, born on October 3, 1944, formed the band.

The couple founded Siegfried & Roy at the Mirage Resort and Casino, which was recognized as the most-visited act in Las Vegas, Nevada, from February 1, 1990, until Horn's career-ending accident on his birthday on October 3, 2003.

Fischbacher and Horn were executive producers of the animated sitcom Father of the Pride from August 2004 to May 2005.
