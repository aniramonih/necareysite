Terrence Dashon Howard is an American actor, rapper, singer-songwriter and record producer. Has first leading roles in the movies Dead Pres President and Mr. Holland's Opus 1995, Howard emerged in the mainstream with a series of television and film roles from 2004 to 2006. He was nominated for the Best Actor Oscar for his role in Hustle & Couler. Howard has starred in many other films including Winnie Mandela, Ray, Lackawanna Blues, Crash, Four Brothers, Big Momma's House, Get Rich or Die Tryin ', Idlewild, Biker Boyz, August Rush, The Brave One and Prisoners. Howard played James Rhodes in Iron Man and his video game adaption, but was replaced by Don Cheadle in the sequels and the Marvel Cinematic Universe. He played the main character Lucious Lyon in the TV series Empire. His first album, Shine Through It, was released in September 2008 .
