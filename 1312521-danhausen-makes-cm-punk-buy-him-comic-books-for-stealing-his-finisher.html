Danhausen Makes CM Punk Buy Him Rare Comic Books For Stealing His Finisher

 Posted on August 23, 2024  |  1 minutes  |  207 words  |  Tandra Barner

bought Danhausen a lot of comic books.

In a video Danhausen posted to his YouTube channel, he was in Chicago for C2E2, and he noted that he wanted to make CM Punk pay him for monies for using his finishing maneuver. After bumping into RJ City, who encouraged him to move on to phase two of his plans for his AEW run, he visited a comic booth at the convention and approached Punk, saying that he owes him money. When Punk asked him whether he was interested in the books or how much they cost, the “Very Nice, Very Evil” one said ” a little bit of both.

Danhausen picked off “Giant-Size X-Men #1”, a highly valuable item because it features the first appearance of the X-Men. The comic was priced at $4,500. He then chose the first appearance of Wolverine, another classic, which was valued at $3,500, and the debut of the Punisher ($6,000) and a classic Black Adam book ($3,800), to which Punk said, “Nobody likes Black Adam.” He then picked a classic Spider-Man book, which was valued at $25,000, and Punk begrudgingly paid for it all. Danhausen said they’re even, and Punk responded by saying he hates him.
