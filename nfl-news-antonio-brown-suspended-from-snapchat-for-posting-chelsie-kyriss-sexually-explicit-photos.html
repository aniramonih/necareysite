Antonio Brown suspended from Snapchat for posting Chelsie Kyriss sexually explicit photos

 Posted on August 21, 2024  |  3 minutes  |  488 words  |  Kelle Repass

Former NFL wide receiver, Antonio Brown is turning into a gift that keeps on giving. Since his time off the gridiron, he has kept himself busy, creating controversies at ease.

Whether it be taking digs at Tom Brady or his former colleagues, he seems to have done it all in recent times. This time, though, Antonio Brown took things to another level by posting an explicit picture on the social media site, Snapchat.

Antonio Brown allegedly posted a picture of Chelsie Kyriss, the mother of his four children performing oral sex on him in bed. The picture instantly went viral before it was eventually taken down after users, and Kyriss in particular reported it to Snapchat.

Following that, it has now emerged that Snapchat has officially suspended Brown’s account from the platform, but not before multiple complaints went in. According to the reputed news outlet, TMZ, Snapchat officials claimed that sexually explicit content is prohibited from the platform and an investigation has been launched.

Antonio Brown’s relationship with the mother of his children has gone downhill in recent months

“Very well aware of what’s being posted on snap. I am not in control of his action,” Kyriss wrote on her Instagram story. “I have asked multiple times to have this part of our relationship to remain private but he refuses.”

Chelsie Kyriss, also slammed the former NFL star by posting a brutal message on her Instagram account. She also accused Snapchat of letting Brown post things despite her numerous reports.

“I have reported his page and all pictures unfortunately Snapchat is allowing his to repost. I am very sorry for any of your kids that follow him and used to look at him as a role model do not condone these actions as you are aware I have kids in involved as well.”

This is not the first time Antonio Brown has taken his online shenanigans too far in recent times. The 34-year-old has been extremely vocal against Tom Brady during the quarterback’s divorce from Gisele Bündchen, wherein he even posted explicit images of the Brazilian model.

Antonio Brown’s last appearance on the NFL field came in January last year against the New York Jets. He ripped off his playing gear and shirt, and appeared on the pitch, following which his contract with the Buccaneers was ripped off. Since then, he has channeled most of his energy into creating online controversies, rather than making an effort to get back onto the pitch.
