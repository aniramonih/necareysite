Julia Duffy Bio | Wiki

Julia Duffy (Full Name: Julia Margaret Duffy) is a veteran American film, stage, and television actress best recognized for appearing in the hit TV series Newhart as Stephanie Vanderkellen from 1983 to 1990.

Her Stephanie role won her three Viewers for Quality Television Awards, seven Primetime Emmy Award nominations, and a Golden Globe Award nomination for Best Supporting Actress. Julia kicked off her acting by performing minor guest roles. Her first official main role was in the series The Doctors as Penny Davis from 1973 to 1978.

Additionally, Julia has appeared in television series such as Designing Women (1991–1992) as Allison Sugarbaker, Shameless (2011–2013), Looking (2014–2015), and Camp X-Ray (2014). Her passion for acting began when she was an adolescent and often appeared on the local stage in Minneapoliss’s Old Log Theatre. During her free time, Julia loves traveling, reading, and spending time with her family.
