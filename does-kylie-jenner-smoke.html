Does Kylie Jenner smoke?

 Posted on August 9, 2024  |  2 minutes  |  413 words  |  Tandra Barner

The Kardashian-Jenner clan has been stealing headlines for years, thanks to their constant presence on social media and extravagant millionaire lifestyles. They are no strangers to controversies, either, covering a wide range of subjects. From controversial Pepsi ads to their choice of partners, this influential family knows how to stay relevant, thanks in part to their matriarch, Kris Jenner.

Among the clan, Kylie Jenner, the youngest member, carries significant weight on her shoulders, given the immense popularity of her siblings. However, it comes as no surprise that she has surpassed all expectations of success. From launching her own multi-million-dollar company to fulfilling her lifelong dream of becoming a young mother — Kylie has achieved it all. Recently, she has been keeping a low profile amid rumors linking her to Timothée Chalamet and speculations about her smoking habits. So, let’s address the burning question: Does Kylie Jenner really smoke?

Was Kylie Jenner caught smoking?

Speculation about smoking habits has long fascinated fans of the Kardashian-Jenner family. Although none of the Kardashians or Jenners have openly admitted to being smokers, they have been frequently seen with cigars, cigarettes, and other smoking paraphernalia. Recently, Kylie was spotted in public with Timothée Chalamet, holding a pack of cigarettes, reigniting the debate once again.

If you recall, Kylie has previously posted pictures on Instagram showing herself smoking cigarettes, using vapes, and even smoking a blunt. With the increasing number of photos surfacing showing the youngest Jenner engaging in this activity, it’s reasonable to suspect that she may indeed be a smoker, although it remains unconfirmed.

On the other hand, while Kylie has been spotted smoking on multiple occasions, most of her sisters appear to participate in such activities only recreationally. As for the family matriarch, Kris Jenner, she has been a known smoker since the beginning of Keeping Up with the Kardashians and we’ve even witnessed Kendall Jenner and Caitlyn Jenner attempting to help her quit by playing pranks on Kris.

Regardless of whether she smokes or not, the chances of ever getting a confirmation are slim, as many celebrities are hesitant to disclose their smoking habits. However, one thing remains certain: Kylie, along with Jenna Ortega, may be the newest additions to the list of celebrity smokers.
