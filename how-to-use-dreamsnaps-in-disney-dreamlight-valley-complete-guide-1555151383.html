How to Use Dreamsnaps in Disney Dreamlight Valley? 

In Disney Dreamlight Valley, players have the exciting opportunity to unlock a feature called DreamSnaps. Once they have acquired DreamSnaps, they gain access to a special in-game camera that allows them to take captivating photos of their adventures and experiences within the magical valley.

Article continues below advertisementArticle continues below advertisement

To use the in-game camera, players simply need to activate it while exploring the valley. They can then frame the perfect shot of their favorite Disney and Pixar characters, stunning landscapes, or memorable moments. After capturing a photo they love, they can proceed to the DreamSnaps menu, which can be accessed by pressing the designated button "D" on PC (the specific button may vary on other platforms).

In the DreamSnaps menu, players are presented with an option to submit their recently taken picture to the DreamSnaps network. This network serves as a platform where players from all around the world can share their extraordinary snapshots, showcasing their creativity and unique perspective of the valley.

By agreeing to submit their photo, players contribute to the vibrant DreamSnaps community, allowing others to see and admire their photographic talents. It also enables them to discover and enjoy the stunning images captured by fellow players, adding a sense of camaraderie and inspiration to the gameplay experience.

How to Enter a DreamSnaps Challenge in Disney Dreamlight Valley?

Here's the information rewritten in separate bullet points:

  • To access the DreamSnaps event challenges in Disney Dreamlight Valley, players must first find and unlock the character Vanellope.
  • Once Vanellope is located, players need to complete her first quest called "Faith, Trust, and Pixel Dust."
  • This quest is a prerequisite for Vanellope to become a permanent resident of the Valley.
  • After successfully completing Vanellope's quest and making her an official resident, players will unlock the full functionality of the DreamSnaps feature.
  • With Vanellope now part of the Disney Dreamlight Valley, players can fully enjoy the DreamSnaps feature, capturing and sharing their magical experiences through in-game photography.

How Does Dreamsnaps Work?

Every Wednesday at 1 PM UTC, players in Disney Dreamlight Valley can look forward to a fresh weekly DreamSnaps challenge introduced in the Event tab. These challenges will be centered around specific themes and will task players with using furniture or clothing items to complete them.

Article continues below advertisementArticle continues below advertisement

For instance, a challenge might prompt players to incorporate playful or familiar furniture items into their snapshots. It's a fantastic opportunity for players to showcase their creativity and design skills while participating in the enchanting world of DreamSnaps photography.

Participating in DreamSnaps Challenges:

To take part in the weekly DreamSnaps challenges, players need to design either a perfect outfit or a beautifully decorated setting that aligns with the theme of the week. They can achieve this by utilizing the Photo Mode feature available in the game.

Submitting the Perfect Shot:

Once players have captured the ideal snapshot that meets the challenge requirements, they can easily submit their photo directly from within the Photo Mode.

Article continues below advertisement

Filtering Items in the Inventory:

To help players find items that fit the specific DreamSnaps challenges, they can navigate to their Inventory and select the All category. From there, they can use the small toggle button located next to the Filters button to filter items based on the required tags.

Ensuring Challenge Requirements:

To ensure their submission meets the challenge criteria, players should strategically place the necessary items close together in the scene to capture them all in one shot. Paying attention to both mandatory and suggested item tags is crucial, as they significantly influence the final challenge score.

Scoring and Rewards:

The primary goal is to attain the highest score possible, as higher scores result in the accumulation of more Pixel Dust. Accumulated Pixel Dust contributes to players leveling up on the DreamSnaps level track, granting them access to various rewards such as furniture and Moonstones.

How to Submit Dreamsnaps?

After unlocking DreamSnaps, players can effortlessly submit their photos by utilizing the in-game camera.

  • After taking a photo, players can access the DreamSnaps menu by toggling it using the "D" key on PC (or the corresponding button on other platforms).
  • Within the DreamSnaps menu, players have the option to agree to submit their picture to the DreamSnaps network.
  • It's essential to be mindful that once a photo has been submitted, editing or resubmitting it is not possible.
  • Therefore, players should carefully review their photo and ensure they are satisfied with it before hitting the submit button.
  • Making sure to submit their best shot will increase the chances of receiving positive feedback and engagement from the DreamSnaps community.

Disney Dreamlight Valley Gameplay

Disney Dreamlight Valley is set in the magical realm of "Dreamlight Valley," a captivating land consisting of diverse biomes inhabited by beloved Disney and Pixar characters.

Article continues below advertisementArticle continues below advertisement

The game embraces a nonlinear gameplay style akin to Nintendo's Animal Crossing series, allowing players to assume the role of a customizable human character residing in the valley. An intriguing aspect of the game is that the valley's time is synchronized with real-world time based on the player's console or computer settings.

In this enchanting world, players embark on adventures to explore the valley, collect resources, and employ their magical abilities to dispel malevolent "Night Thorns," dark magical plants that infest the valley. To sustain themselves and restore their magical energy, players can cook various meals using a cooking station like an oven or a campfire.

Furthermore, other resources can be transformed into materials or furniture at crafting stations. Food consumption replenishes the player's energy, which is crucial for using magic-infused tools like pickaxes, shovels, fishing rods, and watering cans. Consuming specific meals grants additional benefits, such as increased movement speed, better luck in obtaining resource bonuses, and the ability to glide on magic, effectively sprinting across the valley (albeit at an energy cost).

Article continues below advertisement

Furnishing the valley or the player's house with furniture and objects is an engaging aspect of the game. Players can arrange and rearrange these items according to their preferences, adding a personal touch to their surroundings. Furthermore, players have the freedom to move and rotate buildings within the valley, allowing for a customized landscape.

The game incorporates a "Touch of Magic" feature that enables players to customize clothing and furniture designs, further enhancing their character's appearance and style. Unlike in Animal Crossing games, players' tools, clothing, and furniture do not occupy inventory space. Instead, tools are selected using an option wheel and are not subject to wear and tear, ensuring seamless gameplay.

Article continues below advertisementArticle continues below advertisement

Throughout the game, players can earn "Star Coins" currency by selling items to Goofy at various stalls spread across the valley. These Star Coins can be used to purchase crops, seeds, materials, exclusive foods, clothing, furniture, buildings, and upgrades from various characters like Goofy, Kristoff, Remy, and Scrooge McDuck. Players can expand their valley by investing in building upgrades or wells that facilitate fast travel between biomes.

Article continues below advertisement

Interaction with the charming villagers, including both heroes and villains, is an integral part of the game. Players can foster friendships with these characters by engaging in daily conversations, giving gifts, completing quests assigned by them, and participating in activities together. As friendship levels increase, players unlock rewards such as Star Coins, exclusive furniture, clothes, motifs for "Touch of Magic," and increased resource rewards during activities with the villagers.
