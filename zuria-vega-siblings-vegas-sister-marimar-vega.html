She’s an actress, film producer and singer, who’s appeared in more than 20 movies and TV series, while she’s perhaps known best for her portrayal of Julieta, the main character in the comedy series “Mi Marido Tiene Familia”.

It was created by Pablo Ferrer Garcia-Travesi, also starred Daniel Arenas and Diana Bracho, and follows Julieta and her boyfriend Robert, who are renting an apartment from a highly sociable and eccentric family; the series aired from 2017 to 2019, and won one of the 22 awards for which it was nominated.

Zuria played the supporting character Azucena in the crime drama “Mujeres Asesinas”, and starred as Estrella Marina Briceno in the 2010 drama “Curse by the Sea”. It also starred Gabriel Soto and Laura Flores, and follows Luciana whose father has just died, while she’s found comfort in love with a local man.

A couple of Zuria Vega ’s following roles were in the 2013 comedy “Que Pobres Tan Ricos”, the 2015 drama “Ask God for Forgiveness… Not Me”, and the 2016 drama “Simply Maria”.

Most recently, in 2021 she starred as Juana Manuela, alongside Renata Notni and Oka Giner, in the popular drama “The Five Juanas”. It follows five women who bear the same birthmark, and have teamed up to discover the truth behind it.
