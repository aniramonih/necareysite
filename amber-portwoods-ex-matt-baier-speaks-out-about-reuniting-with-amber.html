Amber Portwood's Ex Matt Baier Speaks Out About Reuniting With Amber

 Posted on August 6, 2024  |  2 minutes  |  398 words  |  Patria Henriques

Amber Portwood‘s second baby daddy Andrew Glennon is not exactly well-liked in the Teen Mom fandom. But his villain status doesn’t come close to her previous ex, Matt Baier.

Though many would never have expected them to reunite, Matt’s latest update has fans shocked.

Instagram @mattbept

Amber Portwood‘s ex boyfriend Matt Baier has been controversial from the start. In addition to being significantly older than her, Matt was disliked by fans for his toxic relationship with Amber, and many believed he was just using her to be on TV.

Since their breakup, Matt has been trying to stay relevant in different ways, including releasing a tell-all. But he was overshadowed by the ongoing custody battle between Amber and her second baby daddy, Andrew Glennon.

But now, Matt is speaking out about potentially reuniting with Amber.

Source: Instagram @realamberlportwood1__

“I just now spoke to Amber about the house thing,” Matt shared on Twitter, shocking fans. In the tweet, he is referring to footage where Amber returned to her home with Andrew, which he had trashed, and which she and Gary said he’d left smelling of urine.

“First time we have talked in quite a while. It was great catching up and I think 5 years has been long enough without talking. She agreed,” he wrote.

Unsurprisingly, Matt is trying to cash in on this.

“1 interview only,” he wrote. “DM me if interested.”

“What she needs is someone who actually knows the real her to speak,” Matt continued in a following tweet. “Ppl see a small sample of reality stars lives. What is shown is what sells.”

“I promised I’d only talk when shew as ok with it,” he wrote. “So if ppl want to hear it I’m ready. 1 time only. If they don’t then that’s fine too.”

Fans immediately started speculating once this was shared on reddit.

“I wouldn’t be surprised at all if they get back together lmao,” one user wrote.

Many wanted it to happen!

“Not going to lie, if Matt and Amber get back together that’s reality TV I would 100% tune in for. The absolute chaos.”

Another wrote, “He’s a sleaze but they were perfect for each other. That time period was peak amber. What I wouldn’t give for a round 2.”

One thing fans could all agree on was how disturbing the state of Amber’s home was when Andrew left it.
