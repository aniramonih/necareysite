Who are Harry Styles's other ex-girlfriends?

From 2017 to 2018, Harry dated Victoria's Secret model Camille Rowe. The pair remained friendly after their breakup, and Harry even asked Camille for permission to use a snippet from a voicemail she left for his "Fine Line" song "Cherry."
