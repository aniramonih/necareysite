Who is Amara La Negra?

Amara La Negra, born Diana Danelys De Los Santos on October 4, 1990, is a multi-talented American singer, actress, dancer, author, and television host. She gained prominence through her participation in the VH1 reality TV show Love & Hip Hop: Miami, where she was recognized as the breakout star by Billboard. Shortly after the show's premiere, she secured a significant record deal with BMG.

Amara La Negra identifies as an Afro-Latina of Dominican descent and was raised in Miami, Florida. She was raised by her single mother, Ana Maria Oleaga, who immigrated to the United States from the Dominican Republic. Amara La Negra proudly embraces her Afro-Latina heritage.
