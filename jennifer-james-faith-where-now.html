Who is Jennifer Faith and where is she now?

 Posted on August 18, 2024  |  3 minutes  |  438 words  |  Tandra Barner

A WIFE'S murder plot was revealed when she and her boyfriend were arrested for her husband's homicide.

Although James 'Jamie' Faith's death appeared to be unexpected, Jennifer Faith and Darrin Lopez were found responsible.

Who is Jennifer Faith and where is she now?

On June 21, 2022, Jennifer Faith was sentenced to life in prison.

The Oak Cliff, Texas native was also ordered to pay $6,500 in restitution to her late husband Jamie's family.

Faith was also ordered to pay an additional $250,000 fine.

Previously, on February 7, 2022, Faith pleaded guilty to interstate commerce in the commission of murder-for-hire of Jamie.

Originally, she was charged with the abovementioned crime and obstruction of justice.

By agreeing to a plea deal, the obstruction of justice charge was dismissed.

The plea deal also meant she potentially avoided the death penalty for the murder-for-hire charge.

On July 28, 2023, Faith's accomplice Darrin Lopez was found guilty and sentenced to 62 years in prison.

What was Jennifer's husband Jamie Faith's cause of death?

On October 9, 2020, Faith's 49-year-old husband Jamie was gunned down and pronounced dead outside of their residence in Oak Cliff, Texas.

While Faith and Jamie were walking their dog, the latter was shot seven times by Lopez.

During Jamie's homicide investigation, it was revealed Faith had rekindled her relationship with Lopez; they were high school sweethearts.

When they reconnected earlier in 2020, Faith fabricated stories of physical abuse during her correspondence with her ex-boyfriend.

She confessed that she impersonated Jamie and edited pictures of herself with wounds and marks to taunt Lopez.

Faith further admitted that she pretended to be her husband to provoke her ex-boyfriend into killing him.

On January 11, 2021, Lopez was arrested in relation to Jamie's homicide.

The murder weapon was also recovered from Lopez's home in Cumberland Furnace, Tennessee.

On February 24 of the same year, Jennifer was arrested and charged as well.

How can I watch Jamie's episode of 20/20?

Jennifer Faith's orchestration of her husband Jamie's murder is revisited in an episode of ABC's 20/20 titled Happily Never After.

On September 29, 2023, Happily Never After is scheduled to air at 9pm EST via the abovementioned network.

During the broadcast, show anchors Deborah Roberts and David Muir gathers accounts from those who were involved with the case.

Audiences who miss the initial broadcast can check out the episode via Hulu.
