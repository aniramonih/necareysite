Who is Jay Weinberg's wife? Slipknot drummer leaves heavy metal band after 10-year run

 Posted on August 25, 2024  |  4 minutes  |  698 words  |  Billy Koelling

NASHVILLE, TENNESSEE: Drummer Jay Weinberg, who is married to Chloe Howcroft and is the son of longtime Bruce Springsteen drummer Max Weinberg, has reportedly parted ways with his band Slipknot. 

The shocking departure comes after Weinberg spent nearly 10 years with the Grammy-winning heavy metal giants since joining the band in 2014 as a replacement for Joey Jordison.

The news of Weinberg's exit was announced on the official website of Slipknot but the drummer is yet to release a statement about leaving the band at the time of writing. 

Jay Weinberg proposed to Chloe Howcroft in Peru 

Weinberg tied the knot with Howcroft on September 16, 2020, reportedly a year after he proposed to her on a dirt road overlooking the Incan city of Machu Picchu in Peru.

"We’re married! Text 'JayChloe' to 615-488-7779 to send a picture if you’d like to join our @smilebooth wedding guestbook! Weinberg family portrait by the amazing @richeybeckett. #WhatAWeinderfulWorld," Weinberg wrote on Instagram in 2020 while announcing his marriage to Howcroft. 

The drummer also took to social media to share a photo of him proposing his then-girlfriend back in 2019.

Not much is known about Howcroft's personal life but she appears to be active on Instagram, where she has more than 47K followers. 

Howcroft's Instagram bio reads, 'sometimes art, mostly craft'. It also mentions that she lives in Nashville, Tennessee and that she is 'exploring the world with' her husband, Weinberg, and their two dogs Papaya and Pixie. 

Howcroft's most recent Instagram post showed her enjoying Halloween in Las Vegas dressed as Persephone while Weinberg posed as Hades. 

"Second halloween, vegas edition: hades, persephone, aphrodite, and hermes chicken out of fremont street," she wrote alongside a series of photos. 

Why did Jay Weinberg leave Slipknot? 

Slipknot took to their official website to begin the announcement of Weinberg's departure, saying, "We would like to thank Jay Weinberg for his dedication and passion over the past ten years."

"No one can ever replace Joey Jordison’s original sound, style or energy, but Jay honored Joey’s parts and contributed to the last three albums and we, the band, and the fans appreciate it," the band continued. 

"But as ever, Slipknot is intent on evolving. The band has decided to make a creative decision, and to part ways with Jay. We wish Jay all the best and are very excited for what the future holds," it concluded.

The band did not share the exact reason which led to Weinberg's exit after 10 years with the group. The announcement came just a day after Weinberg took to Instagram to share his excitement over how great of a year it has been for him and Slipknot.

"I'm honored to receive the recognition of 'Metal Drummer of the Year' in the 2023 @modern_drummer Readers Poll. Thank you very much to Modern Drummer, the entire MD readership, and everyone who voted!" Weinberg wrote.

"To know that the hard work my big bros in @slipknot and I have put in over these past two years has made meaningful connections with many of you out there is a total gift. Truly thankful for all of you who have spent time with our music," he continued.

"Lars, Art, Brooks, and Dirk — I could never fully express how much admiration and respect I have for each of you. Your work and talents inspire me every day to be the best drummer I can be, and it’s a privilege to be nominated in this category alongside you all," Weinberg added.

"Thank you again for this kind recognition, I sincerely appreciate your support," he concluded.
