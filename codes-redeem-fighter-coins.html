Find out if there are any Street Fighter 6 codes that you can redeem to get free Fighter Coins, outfits, and more.

First Published January 12, 2024, 13:39

Street Fight 6, the latest instalment in the franchise, will take you back to your childhood with a diverse roster of 18 fighters, including legendary characters like Ryu, Chun-Li, Luke, Jamie, Kimberly and more. In addition to this, you will be able to customize your avatar as you want and will be able to choose multiple outfits, alternative colours, stamps, avatar emotes, and gear.

While some of them can be equipped for free, others will require Fighter Coins to be purchased with real money. However, it is not possible for everyone to do that, and thus, like in many other games, players are looking for Street Fighter 6 codes that they can redeem to get free rewards in the game. 

January 12 Update - We've checked for the latest Street Fighter 6 Codes. We will update this page once we get our hands on more information, so keep an eye on it.

Street Fighter 6 Codes: Are There Any?

As of now, there are no Street Fighter 6 codes released by CAPCOM, and there is no option in the game to redeem any as well. However, it won't be surprising if they are released in future updates, and we can expect them to be available via the game's social media handles.

However, keep in mind that most of the codes released will be valid for a limited period of time, so make sure to redeem them as soon as possible.
