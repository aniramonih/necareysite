Brad Panovich Bio | Wiki

Brad Panovich is an American journalist currently working for WCNC-NBC TV in Charlotte, North Carolina as a chief meteorologist for the First Warn Storm Team. In addition, he has been serving as an adjunct Professor for the University of North Carolina in Charlotte since January 2016.

Prior to joining WCNC in January 2003, Brad worked at WWL-TV in New Orleans, the CBS affiliate on the Gulf Coast. While there, he spent more than three years chasing hurricanes as well as rainstorms that frequently flooded the City.
