Career

Lana began rapping when she was hardly twelve years old. It was the hip-hop duo Audio Two (Mike D and DJ Giz) who made a deal with the Atlantic; they will sign with Atlantic only if Lana gets a contract. Soon, Lana started her music career with a stage name called ‘Sparkle’ at the age of fourteen.

After that, there was no turning back for her, as she started to gain experience and got the hang of the hip-hop and rap world. Soon, she released her first studio album called ‘Lyte as a Rock.’ She took many sleepless years to write those tracks, and it was worth it. The album became very famous, and she received much-needed attention from the music lovers.

Lana’s next two albums were notable too, and she received a lot of appreciation for her hard work. Her fourth album ‘Ain’t No other’ was a super hit among the fans, which became her first album to reach the gold status. In total, she has released five studio albums so far, and all did very well. Lana also gave a title track for the video game ‘NBA Live 2005’ by EA Sports.

Lana has her boutique in Los Angeles which specializes in accessories from belts to sunglasses. She also loves being a DJ and has performed for Michael Jordan at his 50th Birthday party.
