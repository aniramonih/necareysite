But, Dr. Nusbacher was intrigued more by world history and after two years at the Royal Military College of Canada, she decided to go for a Ph.D. program in Modern History at the University of Oxford.

Nusbacher’s insatiable thirst for knowledge led her to enroll in different education programs despite having a Ph.D. already. In 1992, while she was still working as the University administrator, she joined the Canadian Armed Forces. Here, she had the responsibility of planning and overseeing the general logistics. She was good at her job and she got promoted to a division staff officer in 2000.
