San Diego's Former Star Jamie Chung Married Bryan Greenberg in 2015

 Posted on August 9, 2024  |  3 minutes  |  592 words  |  Kelle Repass

American Reality star turned actress, Jamie Chung received recognition after making apperances in MTV reality show Real World: San Diego in 2004. Later, she completed its spin-off show, Real World/Road Rules Challenge: The Inferno II as a cast member in show's 2005 season, The Inferno II.

Later, she earned critical acclaim for her lead play in the independent drama film Eden in 2012. The film is about human trafficking and directed by Megan Griffiths.

Who is Jamie Chung Married to?

Aged 37, Jamie has been enjoying a blissful marital relationship with her husband, Bryan Greenberg for five years. He is actor and singer who gained popularity after portraying as Jake Jagielski in the WB series One Tree Hill from 2003–06.

Chung first met Greenberg once at a party in around 2007 and they hit it off. After very respectful and a conversation for a long time, they again met each other in New York and went on date. But after them, they did not talk for a year.

Started Dating After Five Years Of First Meeting

Yes, the lovebirds officially became a couple in early 2012 and it was kind of a five-year process, Chung said. She continues saying: “It really was like missed opportunities, missed timings, different cities and then finally we ended up in the same city together.”

After nearly 6 years of first met and one year of love romance, they announced their engagement in December 2013. One Tree Hill actor popped the question to his fiancee at the Hotel Vitale during a visit to her hometown of San Jame. Before getting down on his knee, he sang a song he'd written about them.

Halloween Wedding Ceremony

After three years of dating, Jamie walked down the aisle with her groom Bryan on 31 October 2015. Their exchanged nondenominational vows in a traditional Jewish ceremony at El Capitan Canyon resort in Santa Barbara, California.

They had launched a three-day celebration which consists of a welcome dinner on Halloween Eve surrounded by 243 guests wearing costumes. During their wedlock, the bride Jamie donned two wedding gowns. And the first one was a long-sleeve white gown with a plunging v-neck by Monique Lhuillier.

And the next one was a short lace dress with a front slit and gorgeous lace by Jonathan Simkhai. Whereas the groom Greenberg stuck with one set in the entire ceremony wearing a navy suit, white button-down and black bow tie. 

How Much is Jamie Chung's Net Worth?

As of 2020, Jamie has amassed a whopping net worth of $5 million. She credits most of her fortune to her successful acting career of over a decade. On the other hand, her husband, Bryan's net value stands at $3 million, which he has acquired throughout his successful acting performances

Chung is best known for appearing in the 14th season of MTV's long-running reality TV show, The Real World: San Diego. Also, she appeared on its spin-off game show, Real World/Road Rules Challenge, and won the completion with her team named Good Guys. Thus, she might be earned $100,000 per episode.

Bio and Background

Jamie Jilynn Chung was born on April 10, 1983, in San Francisco, California, United States. She was raised by Korean-American parents who moved to the USA in 1980. She has one older sister named Mariah Chung. Her family used to run a hamburger restaurant.

Chung graduated from Lowell High School in 2001. Later she attended the University of California, Riverside where she received her graduation with a B.A. in economics in 2005. So far, she holds American citizenship and is of Korean ethnicity.
