Lauren Rautenkranz Bio | Wiki

Lauren Rautenkranz is an American meteorologist. Currently, Lauren works as a meteorologist at First Coast News in Jacksonville, FL, since 2016. Before, Lauren worked as a weekday 4:00 and weekend evening meteorologist and feature reporter at WECT News. Prior to that, Lauren served as a news correspondent at a lifestyle program airing in North Florida titled My Ocala TV.

While at Gainesville, FL, Lauren served as a weather forecaster at WRUF and as a co-anchor, reporter, as well as a weather forecaster at WUFT News. Lauren also interned at WPTV in West Palm Beach, FL, and WCJB in Gainesville, FL as a weather and news intern. She has also had loads of fun co-hosting UF’s 90th Annual Homecoming Parade.
