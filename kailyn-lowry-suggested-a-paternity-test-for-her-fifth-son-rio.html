Kailyn Lowry Suggested A Paternity Test For Her Fifth Son Rio

 Posted on August 28, 2024  |  2 minutes  |  220 words  |  Kelle Repass

Credit: MTV

Who’s your daddy? Kailyn Lowry shared that she was willing to get a paternity test so that her boyfriend Elijah Scott could be sure that he is the father of baby Rio.

The Teen Mom star gave birth to her fifth son last November, but only recently confirmed his birth.

Credit: MTV

Oh baby!

This may not come as a surprise to some Teen Mom super fans, but Kailyn Lowry suggested that she get a paternity test after she gave birth to her first child with Elijah Scott.

The couple welcomed Rio last November, but kept it a secret from the world for almost a year.

Credit: MTV

Kailyn confirmed her fifth son’s birth on an October episode of her Barely Famous podcast.

Soon after, The Sun reported that she had shared photos of her son.

Kailyn showed off the baby during a Q&A on her Instagram.

Source: Instagram @kaillowry

“Who does Rio look like the most?” a fan asked.

“Some of his faces and mannerisms are like Elijah but other than that I think he just looks like himself. I don’t see anyone yet,” Kailyn replied.

In the photo, the baby’s face is mostly obscured by the angle. Despite her son’s resemblance to his dad, the MTV mom was willing to get a test to confirm paternity.
