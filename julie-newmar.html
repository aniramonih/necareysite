Into The Life Of Julie Newmar

 Posted on August 7, 2024  |  3 minutes  |  540 words  |  Martina Birk

The Batman Catwoman, Julie Newmar, amazed every man jack since a young age. Newmar is a multi-talented woman. Further, she is an actress, singer, dancer, lingerie inventor, writer, and real estate expert.

The Li'l Abner (1959) starlet was born as Julia Chalene Newmeyer on 16 August 1933 in Los Angeles to her father Donald Charles Newmeyer and her mother Helen M. Jesmer.

Who Was The Father of Streetwalkin' Actress?

Donald Charles Newmeyer, also known as Don Newmeyer, was a former American Football Player. Moreover, he was a coach for Gridiron Football.

In addition to this, Charles was a Physical Education head at LA City College. 

When Did The Real Estate Tycoon Graduate?

Since her infancy, the Deep Space diva was interested in learning ballet, dance, and piano. Newmar completed high school when she was 15 years old. The Beautiful Darling lady actress attended John Marshall High School.

In her later years, the Hysterical actress joined the University of California at Los Angeles to sharpen her knowledge of classical piano French, and philosophy.

Julie Newmar's Networth

As of 2022, Mackenna's Gold starlet has a total wealth of $110 million ensued from her lifelong acting career. This ensures that Newmar was a high-paying actress of her time.

The Nobody's Body singer is living in her lavish house in Los Angeles at present. The Gotta Move songster writes short stories and magazine articles which also contribute to her tremendous wealth.

Is The My Living Doll Actress Married?

The bi-monthly memoir writer married J. Holt Smith on 5th August 1977. Smith was a lawyer by profession. Soon, the loving couple got a son, John Jewl Smith.

However, the married pair divorced in 1984 due to some personal disputes.

Who Are The Children of The Oblivion Diva?

The Fantasy Island actress gave birth to her one and only kid on 25 February 1981. Jewel cannot hear and even has Down's Syndrome with a few other disabilities.

However, the brave woman loves her son a lot and even supports him in each and every possible way.

Health issues of Julie Newmar

The Defenders actress was a patient of Charcot-Marie-Tooth Syndrome since her birth. This problem has been more enunciated in her old age.

Nevertheless, Newmar is troubleshooting the defect and seeking medications.

Siblings of The Ghosts Can't Do It, Actress

The Monster Squad serial actress has two siblings: John Newmeyer and Peter Bruce Newmeyer. The fashion designer is the eldest child of the Newmeyer family.

Newmar's brother John is a San Francisco-based epidemiologist while the profession of Peter Bruce has not been revealed till now.

Awards & Achievements

The Maggie starlet has been honored by couple more awards. Newmar has received Tony Award for the best actress.

Additionally, the author of Secret Lives of Julie is the winner of the Lifetime Achievement Award (2014), at the California Independent Film Festival.

What Did The Inventor Invent?

Newmar is indeed an inventor. The glamorous woman invented Nudemar, a pantyhose that would relieve the pert feelings in the derriere. 

The product was made from Monsanto which was stretchable and even contained a seam in the middle. Newmar filed a patent for Nudemar in the year 1975.
