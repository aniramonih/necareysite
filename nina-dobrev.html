Nina Dobrev: Bio, Wiki, and Early Life

Nina Dobrev was born on 9th January 1989 in Sofia, Bulgaria. Her parents are Konstantin Dobrev, a computer specialist, and Michaela Constantine, an artist. She further has one older brother, Alexander Dobrev, born in 1984.

Dobrev studied at the J. B. Tyrrell Sr. Public School, located in Scarborough, Canada. Then, she went to study at the Wexford Collegiate School in sociology for the Arts, Scarborough, Ontario. However, she dropped out in 2006 to start her acting work.
