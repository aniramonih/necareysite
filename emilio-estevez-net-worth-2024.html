Early Life and Career Beginnings

Emilio Estevez was born in New York City to actor Martin Sheen and artist Janet Templeton. Estevez’s acting career began in the early 1980s, with roles in television films and series. His breakthrough came with the film “The Outsiders,” which catapulted him to fame.
