Reggie Millers Wife and Children: An Intimate Look Into His Personal Life

 Posted on August 24, 2024  |  3 minutes  |  485 words  |  Tandra Barner

Following his divorce from his first wife, Reggie Miller started a new chapter in his life, building a beautiful family with his second wife. The renowned Indiana Pacers player is prominently featured in the latest Untold sports documentary on Netflix, particularly in the in-depth exploration of the historic Malice At The Palace, which involved players like Ron Artest, Ben Wallace, and Stephen Jackson.

Marita Stavrou, a well-known American actress and media personality, rose to fame in the 1990s with blockbuster entertainment series such as “Strictly Business” and “Family Matters.” Notably, she was Reggie Miller’s first wife, and they were married from 1992 to 2001. With the release of the documentary, fans have expressed a keen interest in learning more about Reggie Miller and his family.

Meet Laura Laskowski: Reggie Miller’s New Wife

Laura Laskowski is Reggie Miller’s current wife, and they got married after his divorce from Marita. Their relationship blossomed following the end of Miller’s first marriage. As of January 2024, they have been together for 20 years. Laura has maintained a low profile, preferring to stay out of the public eye and leaving media appearances to her spouse.

Reggie Miller’s Children: A Growing Family

Reggie Miller and Laura Laskowski have three children together – Ryker, Lennox, and their youngest, whose name hasn’t been disclosed. Their son, Ryker, was born on August 29, 2013, while Lennox came into the world on May 25, 2016. Their third child was born in January 2021. Initially, the couple kept their son’s identity and appearance private, with Laura revealing his identity through a snapshot posted in July 2015 and disclosing it in December 2016.

Ryker, their eldest child, seems to be enjoying his childhood alongside his siblings. Meanwhile, their second child brings even more joy to their growing family. The names of their elder children and their youngest remain undisclosed. Based on their Instagram photos, Miller’s children are thriving and evidently enjoying their upbringing.

Reggie Miller’s Children: Embracing Sports

With a father deeply rooted in the world of sports, Reggie Miller’s children have naturally developed a fondness for various athletic activities. Their eldest, Ryker, demonstrates a strong interest in basketball and has displayed notable skills from a young age. Notably, he achieved significant success in basketball at the age of eight in 2021. Additionally, he actively participates in football and tennis. Lennox, their daughter, shares her brother’s love for sports, particularly showing interest in playing soccer.

Reggie Miller’s Relationship Timeline with Marita Stavrou

Reggie Miller’s first marriage was with actress Marita Stavrou, who gained recognition through her work in the entertainment industry. The former couple’s relationship, introduced by the legendary Magic Johnson, culminated in marriage in 1992. Unfortunately, their union ended in divorce on April 2, 2001. The divorce coincided with Miller’s signing of a $36 million deal with the Indiana Pacers, and the legal proceedings were highly publicized and marked by contentious disputes over financial matters.
