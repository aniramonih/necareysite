Omari Hardwick Bio

Omari Latif Hardwick was born on January 9th , 1974 in Savannah, Georgia. His first name Omari stands for the highest reputation. His middle name, Latif, means gentle.

Hardwick attended the Marist School in Atlanta, Georgia, where he excelled in several athletic endeavors. He was so good at football that he received scholarship offers from several colleges. He chose Furman University in South Carolina, where he played as a defenseman. Latif spent a year at Furman before transferring to the University of Georgia Athens (UGA), where he graduated in 1996.

After graduation, Omari Hardwick tried his hand at the NFL, but his career was cut short by a knee injury. When his footballing dreams fell through, Hardwick (who always had a flair for poetry and theater) wondered what to do with his life. Under divine guidance, he decided to engage in acting.

He moved to New York City, where he took acting classes at the Beacon Theater. He also performed at various small theaters in New York. Hardwick struggled for some time and had to work odd jobs to survive. This was until 2004, when he landed a role in the TV movie, Sucker Free City . Since his humble debut, the Georgia native has never looked back.

He has written at least 4000 poems and has participated in several poetry slam competitions.

Wealth (income and earnings)

The American actor has an estimated net worth of $1.5 million. For an actor who was homeless and eventually lived off his car, Hardwick did really well. The actor made his fortune from his acting performances as well as his business ventures. He has a production company called Bravelife Films. Despite his busy career, Hardwick is still an avid poet.

parents and brother

Omari Hardwick comes from a family of six. His father’s name is Clifford Hardwick IV while his mother’s name is Joyce. His father is a lawyer while his mother stayed at home. The actor also has two brothers (Jamil and Malik) as well as a sister named Shani Hardwick. Hardwick remains very close to his siblings. One of his brothers lives with him at his LA base and the other lives in Atlanta. His brothers also use their social media accounts to promote his various projects.
