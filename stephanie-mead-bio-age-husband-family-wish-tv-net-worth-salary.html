Stephanie Mead Biography

Stephanie Mead is an American meteorologist best known for serving as the morning weather anchor and traffic anchor for WISH-TV’s Storm Track 8 Weather broadcast. She is also widely recognized for her work in Northern Television Centre, KMVT-TV, and the NBC 26. Her reporting skills on NBC 26 earned her a Chicago/Midwest Regional Emmy Awards nomination in 2017.

Born and raised in Chicago, Stephanie attended Hinsdale South High School in Darien, Illinois, and later enrolled at Northern Illinois University in Dekalb in 2009. In 2013, she graduated with a Bachelor of Science degree in Geography with a minor in journalism.

While still in college, Mead joined the Northern Television Center in Dekalb, serving for two years before her graduation. At the station, she sharpened her broadcast skills by serving as a weather anchor and reporter. She also spent three months working as an intern at the ABC’s affiliate station WTVO-TV Channel 17 in Rockford, Illinois.

After graduation, Stephanie joined KMVT as a meteorologist, forecasting weather on weekends, and also filling in for the morning and evening shows. Mead also served at the station’s sister channel KSVD-TV as a weather reporter.

In April 2015, Mead moved to Green Bay and joined NBC 26 as a morning weather forecaster as well as a newsroom reporter. Thanks to her excellent performance that she was honored with a nomination for the prestigious National Academy of Television Arts and Science’s Upper Midwest Emmy Award in 2016 and 2017 respectively.

In April 2017, Stephanie joined WISH-TV in Indianapolis, Indiana where she serves as its weather anchor and traffic anchor. She can also be seen broadcasting Daily News 8 Daybreak between 4 and 10 am.
