Middle-Distance runner Colleen Quigley is in a long-distance relationship with her boyfriend

From modeling ramp to racing track, Colleen Quigley has found success everywhere. She has grabbed the spotlight with her super-speed in the racing track. But very little is know about her personal life.

Going through her Instagram, we found that she is in a relationship with a guy for a long time now. Who is he? Let's know more about her relationship status and her personal life.

"It's been nine years now!"

Colleen and her boyfriend Kevin is officially dating each other for a very long time now. As per one caption, we can guess the pair is together for more than nine years now. 

Right after finishing her freshman year, Quigley started her modeling her career. During that time, she already began to date Kevin. In an interview, talking about how she could've chosen a different career and turned things differently in her life, she said,

"Thank God I made the decision that I did because this was the best year of my life. I just couldn't have imagined doing it any other way".

She further said,

"Who's to say, you know, if I chose the modeling thing I would've felt the same way. Who knows? I wish I could do what I'm doing and be closer to my boyfriend or be closer to my family".

Kevin is a secretive person as he keeps his Social media profile on private mode. So, any more details about his profession and his personal life are not disclosed.

She loves cooking and traveling...

As much as loves running, she enjoys cooking healthy dishes. During her free time from training and running, she is seen cooking delicious desserts and foods.

In Kevin's 27th birthday, the couple took the beach trip across Cabo, France, and Aruba. Colleen mentioned about their dating life a little bit in the caption and showered her love for her partner. 

Her Family; Blessing in disguise!

Every time she comes back from her race, Colleen takes her niece to many places while showering her love for her sister's child. 

Her parents and siblings are very supportive of her. For instance, in the 2016 Rio Olympics, all of her family members accompanied her to the race to show their support and love.
