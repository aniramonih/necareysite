Jonathan Silver Scott Wiki Biography

Jonathan Silver Scott, born on the 28th April 1978, in Vancouver, British Columbia, Canada, is a prominent figure in the reality television realm, with roles as co-host, producer, actor, and illusionist in shows such as “Property Brothers” (2011- ), “Property Brothers – Buying + Selling” (2012- ), and “Brother vs. Brother” (2013- ). Scott’s journey took off in 1994.

If you’re curious about Jonathan Silver Scott’s financial status, credible sources estimate his net worth to be as high as $10 million by late 2016, earned through his successful television career. Apart from his own reality show, Scott is the co-founder and executive producer of Scott Brothers Entertainment, a company behind television and film content for Canadian and US broadcasters.

Growing up on a horse farm in Maple Ridge with his identical twin brother Drew and older brother JD, Scott’s passion for the entertainment industry sparked at a young age, thanks to his father’s involvement as an actor and assistant director. In 2010, Jonathan and Drew launched Scott Brothers Entertainment as an independent production company, venturing into television production.
